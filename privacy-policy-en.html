PNR TV - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

PNR TV - Smart Media Player ("we", "our", "app") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This policy describes our data collection, use, and protection processes. By using the application, you agree to the terms stated herein.

2. Data Collection and Storage

2.1. User Account Information

The application stores the information necessary to connect to your service provider (Username, Password, Server URL/DNS) only locally on your device.

IMPORTANT: Passwords and server information are stored using Android's secure encryption mechanism. This information is never sent to our servers or third parties.

2.2. Usage and Experience Data

To personalize the user experience; data such as Favorites, Recently Watched, Playback Positions, and Viewer Profiles are kept on your device. This data is completely private to you and is not transmitted externally.

2.3. Analytics and Performance Reporting

Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics are used for debugging and performance improvement.

Data Collected: Device model, Android version, and anonymous error reports.

Privacy: These services absolutely do not collect personal information (PII) such as usernames, passwords, or DNS.

3. Third-Party Services

The application uses The Movie Database (TMDB) API to provide visual data and content details. Requests sent to TMDB only contain content searches; no personal data is shared.

4. Data Security and Encryption

Industry-standard security measures are implemented to protect your data:

  • Android Security Crypto: Sensitive data is encrypted within the device.
  • ProGuard: Application security is enhanced through code obfuscation.
  • Local Database: All data is stored in Android Room database without being sent over the internet.

5. Data Control and Deletion

All your data is under your control. You can delete your accounts or clear the cache from the application settings. When the application is removed from the device, all data stored on the device is permanently deleted.

6. Legal Notice and Disclaimer

No Content

PNR TV does not provide any media or content. The application is only a player.

User Responsibility

The user is solely responsible for the copyright compliance and compliance with local regulations of the content being watched. This application does not support or endorse the publication of copyrighted materials without the permission of the copyright holder.

Independence

PNR TV has no connection with any third-party content provider.
